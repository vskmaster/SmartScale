Step 1: Get a physical card

Find a standard plastic card (ATM card, credit card, debit card, driver's license, or transit pass).

These cards adhere to the ISO/IEC 7810 ID-1 standard, which guarantees they are exactly 85.60 mm wide and 53.98 mm high.

Hold the card up to your screen, ready to align it with the digital outline in the next step.
